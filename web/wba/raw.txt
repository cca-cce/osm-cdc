Promoting Tourism: All Country Tourism Board Websites in the World (2024)
Dumitru Brinzan
21–27 minutes

Ever wondered how tourism is promoted by different countries and destinations in the world?

I find it interesting to look at the official tourism board websites of countries and major destinations. Many of these websites are run by the ministries of tourism, while a few are run by professional destination management companies.

Either way, it is interesting to look at how different destinations promote themselves, what they focus on, how often they update their offers, etc.

I hope to keep an updated list of official (and some unofficial) tourism/travel websites of all countries/destinations in the world, as well as links to their social media profiles – Instagram, Facebook and Twitter.
What is a Tourism Board?

A tourism board is an official organization that promotes their destination, be it a city, a country, or a region. They will often have a separate website that includes a calendar of events, information about available accommodation, hiking trails, seasonal activities, etc.

Tourism boards are often responsible for organizing familiarization trips (FAM Trips) for workers in the tourism industry, as well as influencer campaigns.
